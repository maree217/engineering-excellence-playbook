## How to Use This Toolkit

### For New Client Engagements

1. **Discovery Call**
   - Use: `sales-materials/competitive-positioning.md`
   - Goal: Understand pain points, position against alternatives

2. **Strategy Workshop** (4 hours)
   - Use: `assessment-tools/ai-maturity-assessment.md`
   - Goal: Assess current state, identify opportunities

3. **Proposal Development**
   - Use: `proposal-templates/level-X-proposal.md`
   - Goal: Scope engagement with clear deliverables

4. **Case Study Validation**
   - Use: `case-studies/` (industry-relevant)
   - Goal: Demonstrate proven results

## Key Differentiators

### vs Big Consulting (Accenture, Deloitte, IBM)
- Senior practitioners only (no junior consultants learning AI on your budget)
- Fixed deliverables and timelines (not scope creep guarantees)
- Working code from day 1 (not months of strategy documents)
- Complete knowledge transfer (not ongoing dependencies)

### vs AI Boutiques
- Microsoft ecosystem expertise with enterprise integration experience
- Production-ready templates (not custom development from scratch)
- Capability transfer methodology (not black box implementations)
