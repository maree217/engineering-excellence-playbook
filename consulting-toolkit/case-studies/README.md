## Using Case Studies in Sales

### Matching Case Studies to Prospects

| If Prospect Is In... | Lead With... | Supporting With... |
|---------------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Banking/Finance | Financial Services | Manufacturing (process efficiency) |
| Healthcare/Life Sciences | Healthcare | Financial Services (compliance) |
| Manufacturing/Industrial | Manufacturing | Financial Services (ROI focus) |
| Retail/E-commerce | Financial Services | Manufacturing (operations) |
| Professional Services | Financial Services | Healthcare (knowledge work) |

### Key Metrics to Highlight

**For Cost-Focused Prospects:**
- Break-even periods (4-6 months typical)
- Annual savings figures
- Staff productivity multipliers

**For Quality-Focused Prospects:**
- Defect/error reduction percentages
- Accuracy improvements
- Customer satisfaction scores

**For Speed-Focused Prospects:**
- Processing time reductions
- Time to first results (2-8 weeks)
- Decision-making acceleration

## Case Study Structure

Each case study follows this format:

1. **Executive Summary** - Quick wins and key metrics
2. **The Challenge** - Before state and business impact
3. **The Solution** - Level 1/2/3 implementation phases
4. **Technical Architecture** - Microsoft ecosystem integration
5. **Results & ROI** - Quantified outcomes
6. **Implementation Journey** - Timeline and milestones
7. **Key Success Factors** - What made it work
8. **Lessons Learned** - Challenges overcome
9. **Client Testimonial** - Social proof
10. **Replication Framework** - Adapt to similar organizations
